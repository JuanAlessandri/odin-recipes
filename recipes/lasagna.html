Lasagna

The world's best lasagna.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna pasta
  • 1/2lb ground beef
  • 1 jar pasta sauce
  • 1lb mozzarella cheese
  • 1lb ricotta cheese
  • 1 Large Onion

Steps

  • Chop onion into small pieces
  • Fry onion until charred bits
  • Add ground beef, cook until brown
  • Add sauce to ground beef and stir until well mixed.
  • Cook pasta 11 minutes in boiling water
  • Layer pasta, sauce mixture, riccotta, mozzerella
  • Make 3 layers, topping with shredded mozzarella.

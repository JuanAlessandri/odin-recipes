Chicken Fajitas

Average Chicken Fajitas

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons of canola or other neutral oil, divided
  • 1 pound chicken tenders
  • 1 tablespoon fajita or taco seasoning
  • 2 large bell peppers (any color), seeded and sliced
  • 1 large red onion (12 oz), cut into 16 wedges
  • 1/8 teaspoon fine salt, plus more to taste
  • 1/4 cup water.
  • 8 small flour or corn tortillas, warmed
  • Sour cream or plain Greek yogurt, for serving (optional)
  • Hot sauce, for serving (optional)

Steps

  • In large skillet over medium-high heat, heat 1 tablespoon oil. Add chicken and fajita seasoning. Cook, tossing with tongs, until the tenders are coated in the seasoning, then spred them out in a single layer in the pan.
